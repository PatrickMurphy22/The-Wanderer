Amsterdam

Amsterdam is the capital and most populous city of the Netherlands


Amsterdam is known for its beautiful canals, quirky architecture, and lively nightlife. It's also a great place to find Dutch art and culture, and the city has a rich history that dates back to the 13th century.


History has it that Amsterdam was first eyed by two fishermen and later grew to be a vibrant port in Europe.


The rich cultural background has propelled museums, art galleries, and theatres to thrive. Amsterdam is the leading city with the most extensive museums per inhabitant in the world.


Amsterdam is the perfect location for backpacking or looking for a city break

Food

Amsterdam is a charming Dutch city known for flowing canals, colorful tulips and wooden clogs. It's also a city where it's possible to spend days strolling along waterways and visiting iconic sites like the Anne Frank House, Rijksmuseum and Van Gogh Museum. But what about the food in Amsterdam?


Are you wondering what to eat in Amsterdam during your first trip to the DAM?


From mini pancakes to croquettes from a vending machine, Amsterdam offers the hungry traveller plenty of unique culinary experiences. Don't go home without trying at least one of these traditional Dutch foods.
